This class define the possible payment flow response with his possible fields.

Parameters

paymentMethod

is the payment method used to make a payment (e.g. Credit, Debit, QR, Link)

paymentReference

is the number that serves as the unique identifier of the transaction.

paymentCreationDate

is the date the transaction was created.

paymentAmount

Is the value of the payment made.

paymentSnDevice

Is the serial number of the device that made the transaction.

paymentBrandName

Is the username registered in the Smart device.

paymentInstallments

Is the number of installments that the user chose at the time of making the payment.

paymentLastFourDigits

They are the last 4 digits of the customer's card that made the payment.

paymentStatusError

Is the field to register transaction problems and errors
